Something we can do for our home

Invest in a good bike and helmet.

When your destination is within 5 to 10 miles,just ride your bike.Develop a habit of riding bicycle to near places. Short trips usually have damage to your car and the environment, riding a bike can give you more exercise, and you will feel better.

Unplug the unused electrical plug

Even if the electrical equipment is off, they still need energy. If you unplug your plug or use a lightning arrester to turn the switch off the wall, you can reduce carbon dioxide emissions by 1000 lbs per year. Get into the habit of turning off the switch before you go to bed.

Planting trees

The trees can regulate the climate and maintain ecological balance. Through photosynthesis, trees absorb carbon dioxide and emit oxygen to make the air clean and fresh. The oxygen released by one hectare of trees is enough for 65 people to breathe

Use a hybrid car

If you're looking for a new vehicle, a hybrid car is an increasingly popular option. There are many kinds of hybrid cars on the market, normal sdean, and also large SUV. These cars not only have less emissions, they also save you money and you do not have to go too far to the gas station.

Use led lights

Replacing the three most commonly used bulbs with energy-saving fluorescent lamps reduces carbon dioxide emissions by 300 lbs per year and saves $ 60. A standard, energy-saving fluorescent bulb reduces greenhouse gas emissions by about one-third of a metric ton, plus prices are more than six times that of incandescent lamps.
Remember!! energy-saving daylight bulbs do contain a handful of toxic mercury. Therefore, in order to prevent additional landfill pollution, proper recovery is necessary.
